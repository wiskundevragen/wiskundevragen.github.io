  • sin(-x)=-sin(x)
  • cos(-x)=cos(x)
  • tan(-x)=-tan(x)
  • sin(2x)=2sin(x)cos(x)
  • cos(2x)=cos^2(x)-sin^2(x)=1-2sin^2(x)=2cos^2(x)-1
  • sin(x)sin(y)=1/2 * (cos(x-y)-cos(x+y))
  • cos(x)cos(y)=1/2 * (cos(x-y)+cos(x+y))
  • sin(x)cos(y)=1/2 * (sin(x+y)+sin(x-y))
